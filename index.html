What is IceBreaker?

Icebreaker is an application specifically designed to provide an option for engagement and fun for Event Attendees. Built on a subscription model, businesses can subscribe to Icebreaker so that they can initiate meet-wide games, like Charades, Skribbl.io and Werewolf.

I believe that, as someone who has sat through many of such events like the Singapore Science and Engineering Fair (SSEF) 2021, and who has participated in many similar projects, whether it be in voluntary at better.sg or on my own, I have the experience necessary to bring this application to fruition. If you want an easy to way to engage with your friends in any event, from Graduation Ceremonies to Football Marathons, support this application!

I am looking for Frontend Developers and UI/UX Designers capable of bringing this application to it's ultimate form and who can work together to develop an application capable of achieving that same purpose as I have delivered above.
