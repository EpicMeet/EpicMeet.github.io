What is Let's Meet?

Let's Meet is an application designed for customizability and engagement, by giving participants the opportunity to bond over a virtual event, through the concepts of Huddle and private chats. The application allows participants to not only listen in to live events as they take place, but also discuss with friends using mutual voice channels, which allows them to engage in the event much more easily by introducing a more human element into the mix.

I believe that, as someone who has sat through many of such events like the Singapore Science and Engineering Fair (SSEF) 2021, and who has participated in many similar projects, whether it be in voluntary at better.sg or on my own, I have the experience necessary to bring this application to fruition. If you want an easy to way to talk to your friends in any event, from Graduation Ceremonies to Football Marathons, vote for this application. Thank you.

I am looking for Frontend Developers and UI/UX Designers capable of bringing this application to it's ultimate form and who can work together to develop an application capable of achieving that same purpose as I have delivered above.
